Vapur is a simulated trading platform with a little twist. On Vapur, players trade with real-time market data against other players in wagered or non-wagered games.

Features

Join Games!

Join Games!

Join public games or create private games to play with just your friends!

Choose from Three Modes

Day Trade

With no comissions on market orders and games being between 1 and 48 hours, this game is suited for the experienced day trader.

Tournament

Consisting of four rounds, players are rewarded for making past each round with the top 20% of players doubling their wager!

Long Game

Most similar to actual market trading, players can play in long games for up to a year, being able set comissions and utilize options.
